Private Preschool — Built With Intention.

Keizer Rugrats Playschool is a structured, play-based private preschool in Keizer, Oregon serving children ages 2–5. As a Certified Family Child Care Home, we provide a calm, consistent, small-group learning environment designed to build independence, emotional confidence, and strong foundational skills.

Our Program Philosophy

Children thrive when expectations are clear and days feel predictable. Our approach blends intentional play, structured routines, respectful guidance, and developmentally appropriate learning experiences.

Structured Rhythm

Predictable daily flow including circle time, small group learning, outdoor movement, creative studio time, and quiet rest.

Positive Guidance

Clear boundaries, modeling, redirection, and logical consequences. We do not use shaming, threats, or corporal punishment.

Health & Wellness

Daily cleaning routines, illness exclusion standards, CACFP-aligned meals, and strong safety compliance.

Small Group Care

Limited enrollment ensures children receive focused attention and meaningful connection.

Daily Rhythm

Our structured preschool day includes:

  • Arrival & Free Exploration
  • Breakfast & Morning Meeting
  • Creative Centers & Small Groups
  • Outdoor Gross Motor Play
  • Lunch & Story Wind Down
  • Rest & Recharge
  • Afternoon Adventures
  • Closing Centers & Family Pick-Up

Each block is designed intentionally to support cognitive, emotional, and physical development.

Safety & Care Standards

We operate as a Certified Family Child Care Home in compliance with Oregon licensing standards.

  • Emergency preparedness & monthly fire drills
  • Strict illness & exclusion policy
  • Safe Sleep compliance (OAR standards)
  • Mandatory reporting requirements
  • Clear tuition & enrollment policies

Full policies are provided upon enrollment.
