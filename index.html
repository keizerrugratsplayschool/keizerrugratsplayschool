About Our Program

Keizer Rugrats Playschool is a Certified Family Child Care Home offering a hybrid preschool and daycare program for children ages 2–5. Founded by a mother and experienced early childhood educator with a Child Development Associate (CDA) credential, our program blends structured preschool foundations with the warmth and consistency of small-group care.

The program operates in a dedicated single-family home designed exclusively for early learning — no one lives on-site.

Our Learning Spaces

Our thoughtfully designed environment includes:

• The Learning Lab – dramatic play, circle time, and group activities
• The Wiggle Room – indoor gross motor play and movement
• Library & Rest Room – quiet learning and nap space
• Fully fenced outdoor play yard

Our Approach

We focus on building strong foundations through:

✔ Play-based curriculum
✔ Social-emotional development
✔ Positive guidance
✔ Consistent daily routines
✔ Kindergarten readiness skills

Daily Rhythm

Each day includes structured learning blocks, outdoor time, independent play, group activities, meals, and rest. Predictable routines help children feel secure and confident.

Parent Communication

We use Brightwheel for daily updates, secure messaging, digital billing, and attendance tracking. Parents stay connected with real-time communication and documentation.
