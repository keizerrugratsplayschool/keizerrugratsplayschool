FAQ

Is this a daycare?

Keizer Rugrats Playschool is a playschool: a structured, curriculum-guided early learning environment. Care is calm, intentional, and designed to support independence, confidence, and readiness — not passive supervision.

Do you use screens?

Yes — very limited and purposeful only (for specific lessons like safety education or guided movement). Screens are never used as background entertainment or supervision.

What ages do you serve?

We serve children ages 12 months–6 years in a mixed-age setting, in accordance with Oregon Certified Family Child Care regulations.

What if my child doesn’t nap?

Children participate in a calm rest period. If a child does not fall asleep after a reasonable rest window, they may engage in quiet, non-disruptive activities while remaining in the rest area.

How does enrollment work?

Families begin by joining the interest list. As openings approach, families review program information and we schedule a conversation. Enrollment is confirmed when both parties feel confident and aligned.
