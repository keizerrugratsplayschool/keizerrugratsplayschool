About Our Program

Keizer Rugrats Playschool is a hybrid preschool & daycare for children ages 2–5. We blend preschool foundations (language, early math, routines, and readiness skills) with the comfort and consistency of small-group care.

Our program operates in a dedicated single-family home used exclusively for early learning — no one lives on-site.

  • Warm, home-like environment with clear routines
  • Play-based learning with intentional teaching moments
  • Limited enrollment so children feel known and supported
