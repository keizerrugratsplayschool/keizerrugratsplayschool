About Our Program

We’re building Keizer Rugrats Playschool as a private early learning environment that values calm leadership, predictable routines, and intentional learning experiences. Children thrive when expectations are clear and days feel secure.

Designed for calm, consistent days

  • Predictable routines with clear expectations
  • Play-based learning with intentional teaching moments
  • Small-group setting so children feel known and supported

Quality & Care Standards

  • Clear guidance: consistent boundaries and respectful redirection
  • Health & wellness: daily cleaning routines and wellness guidelines
  • Family communication: consistent updates and clear expectations

We do not publish a street address online. Location details are shared through the inquiry process.

Programs

Playschool (Ages 2–5)

A small-group early learning environment blending structured routines with hands-on play. Our focus includes language development, foundational concepts, independence, and social-emotional growth.

Foundational Skills

Early literacy exposure, early math concepts, fine-motor practice, attention skills, and classroom routines — taught gently through play and daily repetition.

Family Communication

Clear communication and consistent expectations so families feel informed and confident.
