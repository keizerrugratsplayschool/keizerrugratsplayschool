Private Preschool — Built With Intention.

Keizer Rugrats Playschool is a structured, play-based private preschool in Keizer, Oregon serving children ages 2–5. We provide a calm, consistent, small-group learning environment designed to build independence, emotional confidence, and strong foundational skills.

Our Program Philosophy

Children thrive when expectations are clear and days feel predictable. Our approach blends intentional play, structured routines, respectful guidance, and developmentally appropriate learning experiences.

Structured Rhythm

Predictable daily flow including circle time, learning centers, outdoor movement, creative studio time, and quiet rest.

Positive Guidance

Clear boundaries, modeling, redirection, and logical consequences—never shaming, threats, or corporal punishment.

Health & Wellness

Daily cleaning routines, illness exclusion standards, and thoughtful wellness practices to support a safe learning environment.

Small Group Care

Limited enrollment by design so children receive focused attention and meaningful connection.

Daily Rhythm

Our structured preschool day includes:

  • Arrival & Free Exploration
  • Morning Meeting / Circle Time
  • Creative Centers & Small Groups
  • Outdoor Gross Motor Play
  • Lunch & Story Wind-Down
  • Rest & Recharge
  • Afternoon Centers & Activities
  • Closing Routine & Family Pick-Up

Each block is designed intentionally to support cognitive, emotional, and physical development.

Safety & Care Standards

Families deserve clarity, consistency, and professionalism. Our policies and procedures are designed to support a safe, accountable program.

  • Emergency preparedness and safety planning
  • Clear illness and exclusion standards
  • Safe sleep practices and supervision procedures
  • Mandatory reporting requirements
  • Clear tuition and enrollment expectations

Full policy details are provided as enrollment steps become available.
